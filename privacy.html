Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  •  NIT Kurukshetra Search Engine

Summary: We do not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data — bookmarks, to-do tasks, session info — stays in your browser's localStorage only. Teacher/admin credentials are stored in a local config.json file.

1. Information We Collect

NIT KKR Search Engine is designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect minimal information:

  • Student access requires no login — no personal data is collected.
  • Teachers and Admins log in with their @nitkkr.ac.in institutional email.
  • Uploaded PDF metadata (title, subject, branch, year) is stored in your browser's localStorage.
  • Bookmarks, to-do tasks, and notification preferences are stored locally only.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data stored in localStorage is used solely to:

  • Maintain your session during a teacher/admin login.
  • Remember your bookmarked question papers.
  • Save your to-do tasks and study list.
  • Display notifications for newly uploaded papers.

3. Third-Party Services

This platform uses the following external services:

  • Groq API — When you use Ask AI, your question text is sent to Groq's servers. No personal identity is shared. See groq.com/privacy.
  • Google Fonts — Font files are loaded from Google's CDN. This is a standard web practice.
  • PDF.js (Cloudflare CDN) — Used for PDF text extraction on the teacher dashboard.

4. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Data never leaves your device (except AI queries to Groq).
  • Clearing browser data removes all stored information.
  • No database server or backend stores your personal information.
  • Teacher sessions are automatically cleared on tab close or inactivity.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you have full control:

  • Clear localStorage at any time via browser settings to delete all data.
  • Bookmarks and tasks can be deleted individually within the dashboard.
  • No account deletion request is needed — there is no central account system.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact the NIT KKR development team via the Contact page.
